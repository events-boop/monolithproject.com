# Feature Spec: Next-Gen Megamenu & Monolith Hub

## 1. Megamenu Navigation Overhaul
Transform the standard top navbar into an SS-Tier "Megamenu" mini-dashboard (inspired by Stripe, Apple, ZoomInfo).

### Dropdown: Chasing Sun(Sets) & Radio
*   **Left Column (The Roster):** Episode quick-links (S1E1: Autograf, S1E2: Lazare, etc.) with mini play icons.
*   **Right Column (The Feature):** A cinematic, glassmorphism card displaying the newest YouTube episode thumbnail, with a hovering "Listen Now" CTA.

### Dropdown: Untold Story
*   **Left Column (The Lore):** Links to "The Vision", "What to Expect", "Dress Code", "Private Tables", and "Story Archive".
*   **Right Column (The Conversion Engine):** High-priority ticket card. E.g., The latest Deron B2B Juany Bravo poster with a live "🟢 On Sale Now" status dot and a direct Posh checkout button to funnel sales straight from the nav.

### Dropdown: The Monolith (New Hub)
*   **Left Column (The Concept):** "The Story", "The Vision", "Manifesto".
*   **Right Column (The Visual):** An image/loop of the actual Monolith, pushing users to explore the core philosophy page.

## 2. Dedicated "Monolith" Core Page
A brand new, heavily cinematic landing page explaining the overarching collective.
*   **Hero Section:** Giant Monolith visual utilizing AI-generated vision videos flowing in the background.
*   **Content:** The manifesto, the meaning behind the collective, and storytelling elements.
*   **Tech Stack:** Heavy `framer-motion` scroll-reveals, ambient noise overlays, and magnetic cursors.
